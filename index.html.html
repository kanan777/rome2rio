Oslo Sandefjord Airport (TRF) to Oslo Central Station by train

The train journey time between Oslo Sandefjord Airport (TRF) and Oslo Central Station is around 1h 30m and covers a distance of around 113 km. Operated by Norwegian Railways (VY), the Oslo Sandefjord Airport (TRF) to Oslo Central Station train service departs from Torp stasjon and arrives in Oslo S. Typically 120 trains run weekly, although weekend and holiday schedules can vary so check in advance.

What companies run services between Oslo Sandefjord Airport (TRF), Norway and Oslo Central Station, Norway?

Norwegian Railways (VY) operates a train from Torp stasjon to Oslo S hourly. Tickets cost $14 - $18 and the journey takes 1h 30m. Alternatively, Vaernes Ekspressen operates a bus from Sandefjord lufthavn Torp to Oslo bussterminal every 3 hours, and the journey takes 1h 35m.
